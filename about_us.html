About Us

A'gogo Holidays is one of India's young & upcoming travel and tour operator who originally started under the name and style of Krishna Tours & Travels - a proprietorship concern est. 1993. Feeling the needs of growing business, diversified its business from air ticketing activities into hotel reservations and package tours under the professional guidance and entrepreneurship of Mrs. Manju Rai.

Since its inception, the company has grown steadily. Encouraged by the response of the customers, went ahead the help of dedicated and devoted staff diversified into outbound tours and hotel reservations. The company went on-line with CRS network as the business increased, and presently work on the Amadeus system.

Eventually, membership to ADTOI (Association Of Domestic Tour Operators of India) was sought for and obtained, The company is now an active member of ADTOI and now we handle a number of inbound tourist, to India and Nepal, not forgetting the domestic tourists, who have also been looked after well.

Presently we are recognized agents of:

Govt. Tourism Departments: Rajasthan Tourism Development Corporation (RTDC), U.P. Tourism, Kerala Tourism Development Corporation (KTDC), Goa Tourism e.t.c.

Tourism by Railways: Palace on Wheels and Royal Rajasthan on Wheels, conducted by RTDC, The Royal Orient (Conducted By Gujarat Tourism), Fairy Queen (Run by Indian Railways).

Hotel Chains such as Taj Hotels, WelcomGroup Sheraton, WelcomHeritage, Historic Resorts and Hotels (HRH), Casino Group of hotels, Clarks Group of Hotels, ITDC (Ashok Group), Holiday Inn, Jaypee Group, Quality Inn e.t.c.

International Hotels: We are recognized travel agents by most International Chains of hotels on basis of TIDS (Travel Industry Designator service) issued by IATA. Your requirements for bookings for international hotels can be made through us, instantly, on-line.

Our list of satisfied customers include travelers from organizations such as the World Bank, American Express, Price Waterhouse, Bank Gessellschaft, Ranbaxy , NEC, VSNL, HPCL, State Bank e.t.c.

We are totally committed to travel and tourism and have a team of dedicated professionals whose warmth transcends traditional Indian hospitality. Be it an individual client or a tour group - A'gogo Holidays is committed to provide a warm & memorable experience to each of its clients.
